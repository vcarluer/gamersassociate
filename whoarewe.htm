Who are we?

Gamers Associate is a team of three friends, all hardcore gamers and passionate about the geek/pop culture.

We are working for French IT companies but each of us wishes to bring something new to the field of game, and specifically to video and role playing games.
This is why we decided to create Gamers Associate, as a launch pad for our projects.
The purpose of Gamers Associate is twofold: to become a real game company, and to offer original and fun content.

We hope that our first game, Slime Attack, will meet these goals.
Slime Attack is the result of a long thought process on the creation of innovative and intuitive gameplay mechanisms dedicated to touch screen devices - our objective is not to reproduce an existing game concept with an emulated gamepad.
This is only the beginning, but the experience is already worth it.

As we stated, we are kids of the pop culture, and we are motivated by a desire to share a storytelling experience. So, our next game will surely be more oriented towards narrative, emotions, and atmosphere.
Stay tuned!
