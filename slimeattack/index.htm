About

Slime Attack is a fast platformer game for Android.

Help Slimy, trapped at night in an awful laboratory, to meet its dream: escape to Mexico!

Bounce, jump in flight, master bullet time and try to survive.

This game includes:

  • 80 challenging levels in Time Attack story mode
  • Infinite procedurally generated levels in survival mode!
  • Innovative gameplay especially crafted for touch screen
  • The cutest Slime character in the world
  • Hours of fun
