Help Slimy, trapped at night in an awful laboratory to meet its dream: escape to Mexico!
Slime Attack features a story mode with 80 levels, a survival mode with 4 levels of difficulty, and many unlockable achievements.
The player will face in these two modes more and more deadly traps. He will also need to study its route to complete the level before the time runs without dying.
Levels of survival mode are created randomly by procedural generation for a better replayability and more fun.

The gameplay of Slime Attack has been specially designed to bring new gaming experience to the players on touch screen devices.
Simple at the beginning with a single gesture to play, it will be necessary afterwards to master the bullet time to survive the many pitfalls that await Slimy.
